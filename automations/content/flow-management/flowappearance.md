# Customizing Flow Appearance  

Customizing the appearance of your flows helps you stay organized and improves visual clarity, especially as your workflows grow in number. FlowRunner™ allows you to change the color of a flow's title bar, making it easier to identify and categorize your flows at a glance.  

### How to Change a Flow’s Color  

To change the color of a flow’s title bar:  

1. Locate the flow whose title bar color you want to change.
2. Find the **Change Color** icon on the right side of the flow’s title bar and click it.
3. Use the color picker to select your preferred color. The title bar will update instantly.
